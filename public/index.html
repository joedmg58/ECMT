Electrical Contractor Management Tool

(E.C.M.T.)

All you need to manage all the electrical contractor workflow. Keep al document related to an electrical project up to date.

If you are a user please login. If not, you can register here.

Electrical Panel Schedule

Keep track of all panel schedules of every project. The technician on site will enter the up to date information and everyone on the company can use this data any time that visit the job site. Give the technician the accurate information he needs.

Parking Ticket Reimbursement

The workers can report all the spences on parking right in the precise moment they park. This allow the contractor to reimburst tickets cost on time.

Contractors

List of all contractor with all contact information and other useful data. Record of how many jobs are active with a particular contractor

Job Ticket

Manage all your job ticket online. Avoid using paper forms and keep track of all your time and material tickets with the auto calculation invoice values.
